Figure 4. Interchange intervention results for 12 multilingual prompts. Replacing the dense K projection (16,384 weights) with the sparse circuit (441 weights, 2.7%) during a full 22-layer forward pass produces near-identical output: mean KL divergence = 4.5 × 10⁻⁴, top-1 agreement = 97.6%, logit correlation = 0.9999. Each bar shows the per-prompt KL divergence. All values are within 10⁻³, confirming the sparse circuit is a faithful causal replacement.

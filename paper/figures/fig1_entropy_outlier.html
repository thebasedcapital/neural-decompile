Figure 1. Layer 0 attention K projection weights are entropy outliers across three models from two architecture families. Red bars: layer 0 values. Dashed line: cross-layer mean. Q and K projections are 15–25% below mean; V is within 2%. TinyLlama layer 0 K is 7.3σ below mean (2.51 vs 3.32 bits). The pattern replicates in Llama-2 7B (Q = 2.84, 2.7σ) and Qwen2.5-0.5B (weight std 4.3σ above mean). Layer 0 encodes discrete routing; deeper layers use the full quantization palette.

Figure 3. Head 21 K activations cluster by content category

Figure 3. PCA of Head 21 K projection activations on 72 prompts across 9 content categories (8 per category). PC1 (86.1% variance) separates code/LaTeX (right) from natural language (left), with Russian most negative and Python most positive. CJK languages cluster in the center. Fisher discriminant ratio = 5.94 (full weights) vs. 5.66 (sparse circuit with 2.7% of weights), confirming the sparse circuit carries the classification signal. Ablation (all zeros) collapses all categories to a single point (Fisher = 0.00).

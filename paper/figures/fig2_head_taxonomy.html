Figure 2. Taxonomy of 32 layer-0 K heads by effective rank

Figure 2. Taxonomy of all 32 KV heads in TinyLlama layer 0 K projection, classified by effective rank (dimensions needed for 90% SVD energy). Rank 1-2 heads implement binary gates using 1-2 embedding features. Rank 3-4 heads perform multi-class separation. Rank 5+ heads encode rich, multi-dimensional representations. Heads 6 and 7 are near-duplicates (cosine 0.992) providing multi-path routing of a single newline detector. Heads 2 and 3 are complements (EOS vs CJK, opposite signs). Head 21 (highlighted) is the multi-script classifier verified by activation tracing and interchange intervention.
